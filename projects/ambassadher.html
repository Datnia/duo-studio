AmbassadHer

A network of over 650,000 women who get rewarded for being themselves.

AmbassadHer members get rewarded for posting on social media about products they love, as well as performing other tasks like reviewing and providing feedback for established brands such as Airbnb and Febreze.

We’ve rebuilt the identity from the inside out. This includes the user portal, on boarding process, dashboard and everything on the front-end of the site.

01

art

direction

Using an energetic aesthetic that every influencer is guaranteed to love, we’ve revived the site to make it representative of its users. We developed a friendly brand identity, full of curved shapes and colorful collaged images highlighting influencers and the brands they rep. We’ve avoided using your typical “feminine” styles because AmbassadHer represents women of all styles, shapes and backgrounds. Gotta love that!

02

digital

experience

Most of the user experience happens once the user logs in. We created a dashboard UI that highlights current opportunities available to the user, stats, their profile and more. Featured brands like Tide and FabFitFun are displayed on the dashboard, but if you have something in mind specifically, we’ve also created in in-depth filter UI. Through our easy-to-use dashboard, users are able to complete brand opportunities from step 1 to step 10 until they become the ultimate brand influencer!

03

seamlessly

responsive

Mobile-first. You hear it all the time. But this is something to take seriously when most of AmbassadHer’s users are young female influencers whose careers revolve around their phones! All screens were designed first in mobile, perfected, then expanded. Different components of the website, like the profile section, dashboard, filter and settings were executed similarly to mobile applications.
