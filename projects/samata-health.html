Samata Health

Progressive mental health care at your fingertips

Samata Health is a mental health care SaaS startup specializing in therapy services for individuals, companies and therapists.

We have rebranded, redesigned and developed a dynamic, long scrolling one-page website to highlight its digital platform and employee mental health benefits.

01

a new look

for a new

company

We worked closely with the Samata Health team to establish a visual identity that represents their company goals - personalization, affordability and diversity. We’ve reinterpreted these goals into digital concepts that make the user feel welcome and comfortable, no matter their background.

02

our saas

design

Who says you need a huge website to make an impact? We’ve merged the qualities of a SaaS website with a wholesome health website. You’ll find a smooth scrolling website with a secondary navigation, interactive testimonials, illustrative features and toggles for each audience… all in one page!

03

dev and

integration

The Samata Health team’s goal is to have a clean, new site that will also help their workflow. We wanted to make sure that users (clients, therapists & employers) reach their individual endpoints. Then, each endpoint is integrated into a marketing automation so they can manage new inquiries and subscribers all on one dashboard.
