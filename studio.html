We are Duo Studio

A small studio where we bring creative and functional together as a duo.

our base is in Maryland, though we tend to work across continents from time to time.

We’re a group of individuals with mixed profiles. From artists to strategists, thinkers to technicians, we got you.

Lastly, and most importantly, we work with each
client
as if they are our only client. That’s why each portfolio project is so unique from the other.

If the solution is hard, we pursue it. If the solution is impossible, we’ll find the next best thing. Like we said, we got you.

design

Each website we design is unique, meaning no templates and no recycled work. We want to make sure each client’s vision is represented in its truest form, so we start from the beginning. Our process varies from client to client and is broken up into curated phases. It goes something like this: strategic planning, brand identity, web structure and web design. Don’t worry, we’ll explain all of this to you during the process.

    Strategic Planning

  • Experience strategy
  • User personas
  • Data science and analytics
  • Information gathering

    Website Structure

  • User flow
  • Wireframing
  • Sitemapping

development

Since we’re creative thinkers, our code is always customized. We leverage custom development to make sure your website is supported on all browsers / devices with fun + engaging interactions. Our services are flexible for both your users and your business. We provide seamless integrations + automations to meet your marketing goals, a precise content management system for your growing business and SEO optimization because we want you to be first.

    Custom Development

  • Front-end development
  • Back-End development
  • Responsive development
  • Performance optimization

    Integrations

  • CMS
  • E-Commerce
  • Marketing automations
  • Motion elements

branding

Every company starts with a brand and every brand shapes a user's perception of the company. It's our mission to visually and strategically help establish businesses grow their presence on the web. We'll work closely with you to build your brand tone and identity, which will closely reflect what your company's about.

    Visual Branding

  • Web brand identity
  • Brand guidelines
  • Logo and collateral materials

    Brand Strategy

  • Brand meaning and purpose
  • Messaging and tone
  • Customer/user research

illustration

We want your brand identity to speak for itself, even without words. Illustrations are powerful and unique design assets to set your brand apart from the rest. We’ll assemble inspiration boards, talk through styles with you, and create one of a kind concepts for you to choose from. The final step is simple - we bring your vision to life.

    Custom Illustrations

  • Icon sets
  • Spot illustrations
  • Branding illustrations
  • Simple animations
  • Hand drawn illustrations
  • Banner illustrations

marketing

Our goal is not just to get visitors to your site, but to get the right type of visitors to your site with the right messaging to effectively capture, engage, and convert your target audience and grow your business. We take a 360 digital approach to marketing that starts with research and strategy, where we help you develop valuable and compelling content that suits your brand and connects with your target audience. The work doesn't stop after your site goes live though - we'll also utilize web analytics tools to monitor and continually optimize your website's performance and gain insight into areas that can use improvement.

    Research & Strategy

  • Audience analysis
  • Competitive analysis
  • Value proposition
  • Keyword research
  • Content creation
  • Content marketing

    Analytics & Monitoring

  • Search engine optimization
  • Conversion rate optimization
  • Performance monitoring & web analytics
  • Social media management
  • Pay-per-click (PPC) management
