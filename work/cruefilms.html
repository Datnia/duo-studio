Evolving the Cruefilms brand

Brand Refresh

From vivid lime greens and hot pinks, to mixed typographical treatments, we executed a digital identity refresh to compliment the Cruefilms logo. Taking inspiration from the movement and fluidity in videography, we created an animated logo to bring the brand to life.

An immersive digital experience, from ideation to launch

Responsive Website Design and Development

Through detail oriented site mapping and discovery meetings, we worked with the Cruefilms team to create an interactive website that showcases their case studies. Website highlights include a contact form targeted for specific audiences, a diagonal-scroll portfolio collection and a behind-the-scenes Easter egg.
